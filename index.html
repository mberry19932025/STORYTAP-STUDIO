FAQ

Everything fans ask before they tap.

NFC questions, shipping details, and how the story vault works.

How does the NFC tap work?

Tap the gear with any NFC-enabled phone to open the story vault. No app needed.

Can I update the story later?

Yes. Swap photos, audio, or captions anytime from your story vault.

Is my story private?

Stories are private by default. You control when and who can view them.

What is the turnaround time?

Most personalization ships within 72 hours. Rush options are available.

What is your return policy?

30-day returns on unworn items. Story programming fees are non-refundable.

Can I use official team logos?

Yes, as long as the fan is authorized to use the mark they upload.
