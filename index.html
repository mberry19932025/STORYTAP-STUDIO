FAQ

Fast answers before you list (or wear) it.

Sizing, customization, and how to use the Marketplace CSV.

What kind of “offended” is this?

Satire and political humor—written to get laughs, not to target protected groups. No slurs. No threats. No calls for violence.

Can you make custom slogans for my act?

Yes. Keep it punchline-forward and avoid hate/harassment. If you share your vibe and the audience (club, college, corporate), we can draft “setlist” options.

What sizes do you offer?

Tees run XS–4XL, and most hoodies/crewnecks run S–5XL. Hats and beanies are one size with adjustable fits.

What payment methods do you accept?

For direct checkout (when enabled): Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, Cash App, Venmo, and crypto wallet payments (BTC/ETH). If you buy via Facebook Marketplace, available options depend on the listing type and your region.

How do I upload the catalog to Facebook?

Download the CSV in the Marketplace section, then import it in Commerce Manager as a data feed. Update the link and image_link columns to point at your real product pages and mockups.

Are these affiliated with any campaign or organization?

No. The TShirt Comedian (OffendHop) is parody/satire apparel. Any references to public life are commentary, not endorsement.

Where do I send questions?

Email hello@offendhop.com for custom orders, bulk runs, and catalog help.

Need a custom setlist drop?

Send your comedic vibe + 3 taboo topics you want to joke about (without crossing into hate), and we’ll draft options.
